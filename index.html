How Rhythm works

Simple. Quiet. Effective.

01

Install the extension

Add Rhythm to Chrome in seconds. No account required. No data leaves your device. Everything runs locally.

02

Choose what to block

500,000+ pornography domains and 22+ major Kenyan gambling sites are blocked by default. Add your own in seconds.

03

Get redirected, not judged

When you visit a blocked site, Rhythm shows a calm, affirming page — not a harsh warning. A gentle pause before you choose.

04

Your rhythm returns

Over time, the redirects come less. Your focus sharpens. Your habits realign. You rediscover the person you want to be.
